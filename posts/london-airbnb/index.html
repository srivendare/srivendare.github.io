Airbnb London

Description of Data

Data type

The data is sourced from the Inside Airbnb , a publicly Airbnb data provider. The dataset comprises of three main tables:

listings - Detailed listings attributes including price (continuous), location, room_type (categorical), neighbourhood (categorical), ratings (continuous) among others. reviews - Detailed reviews given by the guests with 6 attributes. Key attributes include date (datetime), listing_id (discrete), reviewer_id (discrete) and comment (textual). calendar - Provides details about booking for the next year by listing. Four attributes in total including listing_id (discrete), date (datetime), available (categorical) and price (continuous).]

Analysis

A few imputations and transformations are made to create the desired visualizations. There were no major inconsistencies or mismatches in the data, but most of the columns/features The required format should be checked and hence were manipulated in a way that their meanings are retained.
